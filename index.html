Research focus

Focused on structure-based drug design, computational chemistry, and biomolecular simulations targeting neuroinflammatory diseases — particularly tau hyperphosphorylation-driven pathways in Alzheimer's disease and related tauopathies. Research integrates transcriptomics, machine learning-based structure-activity relationships, and microsecond molecular dynamics simulations to identify and validate small-molecule therapeutic candidates.
